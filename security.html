DinarDirham doesn't hold or control physical Gold, DinarCoin or Gold Smart Contracts. All assets issued in DinarDirham are bridged transparently 100% to the liquidity provider using blockchain to insure decentralization. All new Dinar Coin and Gold Smart Contract wallets use multi-signature that belong to Gold owners. DinarDirham has multiple layers of proprietary technologies, secure processes and protocols to ensure the safety of our client's assets. DinarDirham servers are located in carefully selected jurisdictions, which reduces political risks, adverse regulatory actions or natural disasters. We take security very seriously. Security is our #1 priority.

Please find below our security practices:

  • Multi-tiered, multi-firewall architecture
  • The site runs entirely over SSL (https)
  • We hash passwords stored in the database (using bcrypt with a cost factor of 10)
  • We rate limit a variety of actions on the site (login attempts, etc)
  • We use SQL injection filters
  • We verify the authenticity of POST, PUT, and DELETE requests to prevent CSRF attacks
  • We check for strong passwords on account creation and password reset
  • Application credentials are kept separate from the database and code base
  • We use separate passwords for each service
  • Employees must pass a criminal background check as part of the hiring process
  • KYC/AML compliance for all our customers
