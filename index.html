GOLD SMART CONTRACT

Gold Smart Contract is our feature that proves ownership of physical gold bullion.

  • Users can procure GSC from other traders using our platform, or can procure GSC from DinarDirham approved bullion providers.
  • Bullion providers can create new GSC upon payment for physical gold. Thus, the GSC created proves ownership of a quantity of bullion, which can be reedemed as physical bullion at any authorized bullion provider.
  • When a GSC is created it is registered on both the Ethereum blockchain and the Bitcoin blockchain, and its existence can be verified on both blockchains.
  • Bullion held in vaults of the bullion providers can be audited anytime to verify the amount held by clients as GSC.
